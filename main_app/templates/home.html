To get started, simply navigate to the "Create Character" page and fill in the form with your desired information. Once you submit the form, you will be navigated to your newly created characters detail page. Once on this page you will be able to see your character's detailed info such as class and accompanying attributes, along with your characters weapon, and gift. Underneath this information you will have the oprtunity to click a button that will generate a short story about your character! You can generate a new story to replace the curent one as much as you'd like until you feel your character has achieved a satisfying ending.
